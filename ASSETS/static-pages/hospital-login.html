    These online tools are handy for getting both a overview of the how local and internal populations are faring. They feature real-time data organized for easy analysis and are maintained by leading organizations both within the Philippines and the world at large.

    Department of Health COVID-19 Tracker

    This is the official and updated COVID-19 Case Tracker. Originally the DOH nCoV Tracker, the new version collates case data and health system capacity accross the Phiippines
    Johns Hopkins University Interactive Map

    The JHU Coronavirus COVID-19 Global Cases Dashboard by the Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University (JHU) is an online dashboard featuring a real-time world map, US Map, and trends tracker

    Citizens Patrol

    About

    Project Moses is a Crisis Response Toolbox that aims to be an all-in-one web dashboard for Filipinos in times of calamity and crisis mainly and should still be relevant even in times of peace. We are starting with a Virtual Triage that could match patients with hospitals. It is a publicly accessible collaboration tool using Information Communication Technology (ICT) meant to connect COVID-19 response actors.
