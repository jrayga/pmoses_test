About Project Moses

Project Moses is a Crisis Response Toolbox that aims to be an all-in-one platform for Filipinos in times of calamity and crisis. It is a publicly accessible collaboration toolbox using Information Communication Technology (ICT) meant to connect crisis response actors.

The tools in Project Moses to meant to address the issues on lack of relevant and real-time information to help stop the wildfire of misinformation and promote information that would assuage fears, understand risk, implement effective social distancing and improve our public health.

Although primarily built in the midst of the COVID-19 Pandemic, Project Moses aims to still be a relevant and useful platform for the public even during times of peace and order.

The toolbox contains the following feature-modules:

  1. A data aggregator of all news from reliable sources both national and local news outlets
  2. A crowd-sourced information about establishments and infrastructure so you know which ones are still open for business and use
  3. A self-diagnostic tool with the latest DOH algorithm
  4. A platform to proactively add information regarding their hospital, COVID cases, hospital needs, hospital staff, hospital bed and ventilator capacity, hospital rating, hospital’s capacity to quarantine and hospital COVID19 readiness
  5. Case tracker dashboards for COVID19 both national and international
  6. A matching tool for potential patients/patients under investigation to hospitals receiving these types of patients and can give care
  7. A platform that connects the Donors with Donations like Relief Goods and Medical Supplies; Sellers and buyers of medical supplies and relief goods; and Lenders and Borrowers of bikes, scooters, van, trucks and the like
